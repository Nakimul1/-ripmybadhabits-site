How We Make Money

RipMyBadHabit (ripmybadhabit.com) is a free resource dedicated to helping people break bad habits. We keep this site free by earning money through affiliate partnerships and advertising. This page explains exactly how that works — because we believe in full transparency.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Some of the links on our website are affiliate links. This means that if you click on one of these links and make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you. These commissions help us keep the site running, pay for hosting, and continue creating free content.

Our Promise to You

Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial content. Here is exactly how we approach recommendations:

✓

We only recommend products we genuinely believe in. If we wouldn't recommend something to a friend, we won't recommend it to you. Full stop.

✓

Content is written first, affiliate links are added second. We never write articles for the sole purpose of promoting an affiliate product.

✓

We will always be honest about drawbacks. If a product has limitations, we'll tell you. If a free alternative is better than a paid option, we'll recommend the free one.

✓

We clearly identify affiliate links. Articles containing affiliate links include a disclosure notice at the bottom of the page.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255). We are required by law to disclose any material connection between ourselves and the companies whose products or services we recommend. This page is that disclosure.
