⚠️ Mental Health & Crisis Resources

We are not therapists, doctors, or licensed professionals. If you're in crisis or need immediate support, please use one of these resources:

💬 Text HOME

🚨 Call 911

Emergency services — if you or someone else is in immediate danger.
