Our story

We've failed at more habits than you.

RipMyBadHabit wasn't born from a success story. It was born from the wreckage of too many broken promises — "I'll quit tomorrow," "this is the last time," "I just need more willpower."

Sound familiar?

The Moment
Everything Changed

We tried the cold turkey approach. We downloaded the apps, bought the journals, and set the alarms. We felt the guilt of relapsing and the shame of admitting we couldn't do it alone. Again.

Here's what we eventually figured out: breaking bad habits isn't a discipline problem. It's a design problem. The strategies most people use — willpower, motivation, shame — are the least effective tools available.

The strategies that actually work — environmental design, behavioral psychology, trigger mapping, replacement habits — are hiding in research papers that nobody reads. So we read them. And then we turned them into guides that real people can actually use.

How We
Make Money

We keep this site free through affiliate partnerships and advertising. When we recommend a product — like a meditation app, a therapy platform, or a book — we may earn a commission if you sign up through our link.

This never affects what we recommend or how we write about it. We will always tell you when a link is an affiliate link. And we will never recommend something we don't genuinely believe in.
